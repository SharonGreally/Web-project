About us

Who are we?

The Passage Environmental Group was set up in 2010. We are a group of volunteers living in the Passage West area, who meet once a week to discuss environmental issues impacting Passage. We are a diverse group including various nationalities; our members are students, parents and pensioners who all share a common interest in protecting our environment.

Our Mission

Our mission can be summed up in our motto Doing our bit for the planet in Passage!. We are passionate about the area we live in and about protecting the environment. Given the huge environmental challenges the world is facing today, it is sometimes hard to know what changes you can do to make a difference. We in the Passage Environmental Group believe as the saying goes charity begins in the home and home for us is Passage. By each of us doing our little bit for the environment, we can make Passage a nice place to live not just for ourselves but the generations to come.

Our Goals

Through our website we want to achieve the following:

  • Raise awareness about the environmental impact of certain projects on the Passage area.
  • Provide information on recycling facilities in the local area.
  • Provide information on ways you can reduce your carbon footprint.
  • Encourage you to get out into nature and develop a greater appreciation for our environment.
  • Foster a greater sense of community by coming together to share our interest in the environment.
